# SWOT Analysis Prompt

Perform a comprehensive SWOT analysis for {{COMPANY}}.

## Instructions

For each quadrant, provide 3-5 items with:
- A clear, specific statement
- Supporting evidence with source
- Impact assessment (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW)

## Framework

### Strengths (Internal, Positive)
Identify competitive advantages, unique resources, strong brand elements, financial health indicators, and proprietary technology or IP.

### Weaknesses (Internal, Negative)
Identify resource gaps, operational inefficiencies, talent challenges, financial vulnerabilities, and product/service limitations.

### Opportunities (External, Positive)
Identify market expansion possibilities, emerging trends, regulatory changes favoring the company, partnership potential, and underserved segments.

### Threats (External, Negative)
Identify competitive pressures, regulatory risks, market disruption, economic headwinds, and technology obsolescence risks.

## Output Format

| Quadrant | Item | Evidence | Impact | Confidence |
|----------|------|----------|--------|------------|
| Strength | [S1] | [Source] | H/M/L | H/M/L |
| Weakness | [W1] | [Source] | H/M/L | H/M/L |
| Opportunity | [O1] | [Source] | H/M/L | H/M/L |
| Threat | [T1] | [Source] | H/M/L | H/M/L |

## Cross-Impact Analysis
After completing the SWOT, identify key strategic implications:
- Which strengths can exploit which opportunities? (SO strategies)
- Which strengths can counter which threats? (ST strategies)
- Which weaknesses limit which opportunities? (WO strategies)
- Which weaknesses amplify which threats? (WT strategies)
