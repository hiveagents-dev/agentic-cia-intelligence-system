# Porter's Five Forces Analysis Prompt

Analyze the competitive forces affecting {{COMPANY}} using Porter's Five Forces framework.

## Instructions

Score each force from 1 (low) to 5 (high intensity). Provide evidence for each score.

## Forces to Analyze

### 1. Threat of New Entrants (Score: X/5)
Consider: barriers to entry, capital requirements, economies of scale, brand loyalty, regulatory barriers, access to distribution, switching costs.

### 2. Bargaining Power of Suppliers (Score: X/5)
Consider: number of suppliers, uniqueness of inputs, switching costs, supplier concentration, forward integration threat, importance of volume to supplier.

### 3. Bargaining Power of Buyers (Score: X/5)
Consider: number of buyers, buyer concentration, switching costs, price sensitivity, backward integration threat, product differentiation, information availability.

### 4. Threat of Substitutes (Score: X/5)
Consider: availability of substitutes, price-performance of alternatives, buyer propensity to switch, switching costs, perceived differentiation.

### 5. Competitive Rivalry (Score: X/5)
Consider: number of competitors, industry growth rate, fixed costs, product differentiation, exit barriers, diversity of competitors, strategic stakes.
